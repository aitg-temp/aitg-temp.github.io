Research Interest

I am interested in temperature and pressure induced phenomena observed in hydrogen bonded system. My research project involves critical understanding of different phases in different conditions through ab initio Molecular Dynamics in organic-inorganic hybrid perovskites (MAPbBr3 , MAPbI3 , FAPbBr3 etc.) which have immense potential to be used as solar energy materials. In this context, I am also interested in the origin of ferroelectricity in some phases of these types of material.

I have also worked on polymerisation of solid acrylamide under high pressure. We have shown the importance of hydrogen bonding network which response to pressure anisotropically leading to topochemical polymerisation. The effect of kinetics also plays important roles as fast application of pressure leads to different polymer. Currently we are investigating these two process of compression (static and dynamic) at room temperature by applying ab initio Molecular Dynamics.

I am also working on solid materials which shows anomalous phenomena under temperature and pressure, for instances, Negative Linear Compressibility (NLC), Negative Thermal Compressibility (NTE) etc.
