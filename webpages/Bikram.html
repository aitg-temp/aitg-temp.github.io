Research Interest

I am interested in simulating photo-induced excited state dynamics in large scale systems efficiently. Since Non-adiabatic dynamics is very crucial to simulate these processes accurately, we are aiming at developing nonadiabatic Ehrenfest molecular dynamics to localized basis sets within GPAW (a Python-based electronic structure code). This will enable us to simulate large-scale systems efficiently with a moderate computational cost.
